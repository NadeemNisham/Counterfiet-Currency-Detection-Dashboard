Welcome to BlindTrust

This is a simple website created to demonstrate the functionalities of our BlindTrust project, which is a IoT device that helps visually impaired people Identify, Classify & Sumup Sri Lankan Currency.

Identify

You must upload a normal image of a currency, then we will predict what the note is and display the prediction.

Classify

you must upload two images, a normal image of the currency and an image of the currency under UV light, then we will predict is the note is real or fake.

Sum Up

you can upload multiple normal images of the currencies, we will identify each note and display the total amount of currency shown.

Identify Currency

In this feature, you must upload a normal image of a currency, then we will predict what the note is and display the prediction. The possible Predictions are Rs.20, Rs.50, Rs.100, Rs.500, Rs.1000, Rs.5000. This is only used to demonstrate the identify Currency feature.

  • Upload a Currency Image
  • Get the Identidication Predicted by the Model

Classify Currency

In this feature, you must upload two images, one of which is a normal image of the currency, and the other being an image of the currency under UV light, then we will predict is the note is real or fake. The possible Predictions are Real and Fake. This is only to demonstrate the classifying currency feature.

  • Upload a Normal Currency Image & a Image under UV Light
  • Get the Classifictaion Predicted by the Model

Sum Up Currency

In this feature, you can upload multiple normal images of the currencies, we will identify each note and display the total amount of currency shown. The possible Predictions are any value greater or equal to zero. In this we dont check if the currency is real, to keep the dashboard simple. This is only to demonstrate the sum up feature.

  • Upload Muliply Normal Images of Currency
  • Get the Total Sum Predicted by the Model
